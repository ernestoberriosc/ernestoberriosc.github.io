About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Biology. I am part of the Stochastic Evolutionary Dynamics group (a.k.a. “The Rescue Team”) led by Hildegard Uecker. My research aims at studying complex phenomena using mathematical models. My research interests include evolutionary dynamics (e.g., antibiotic resistance, population bottlenecks, mating systems) and nonlinear dynamics (e.g., nonlinear waves, pattern formation).
